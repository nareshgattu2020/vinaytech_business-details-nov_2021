The best way to prepare resume
------------------------------------
Go to Naukri, Monster, Indeed etc... Job Portals, verify the Job Responsibilities Companies are 
looking for. Try to keep those points in the resume.

Use your creativity to add some fishy / crazy / new points in the resume which attract the interviewer [those are ansered by you only]


Note:

1) Time to time requirement, responsibilities and combinations are changing.
2) Location to location, the way of project implementation and responsibilities are different.
3) Small enterprizes and MNCs requirements and responsibilities are different.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


You want to make your resume strong, try to place the below topics in the resume: 

Role, Life Cycle, Challenges, Documents, Testing, Performance Tuning, Report Creation Components,  Versioning, Refresh, Scheduling

a) PBI Desktop [RLS, Custom Visuals, Bookmarks, Slicers,Filters, Buttons, Formatting, Modeling]
b) PBI Service Level [Workspace, Appspace, Security, Refresh, Gateway, Incremental Refresh, Upload report,Content Pack, Schedule Refresh, SubScriptions, Alerts etc.]
c) Microsoft Flow specify to schedue the PBI Datasets
d) Power Apps [Providing customer frindly form to enter details which reflect in PBI Dataset]
e) SQL Topics [Joins, Sub Queries, Set Operations, Procedures, Views, Analytical Functions ]
f) Azure Awareness [SQL Database, Data Lake etc.]
g) MSBI-Cubes knowledge
h) Versioning tool knowledge [TFS, PVCS, Git etc...]
i) Testing compulsary
